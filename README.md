# Sales & Profitablity Analysis Dashboard
## 📌 Project Overview
This project analyzes transactional sales data (2022–2023) using SQL and Power BI to uncover insights related to revenue distribution, customer behavior, and product profitability.
The objective is not just reporting metrics, but identifying business problems, inefficiencies, and opportunities for growth.

## 🎯 Objectives

+ Analyze revenue distribution across countries, categories, and customers
+ Evaluate customer segmentation and loyalty effectiveness
+ Identify high-performing vs underperforming products
+ Provide actionable business recommendations


## 🛠 Tools & Technologies Used 
## Python
+ SQL (PostgreSQL) → Data analysis, transformations, and modeling
+ Power BI → Dashboard creation and visualization

## 📈 Dashboard Overview
1. Overview 
+ Total Revenue, Profit, and Margin KPIs
+ Revenue trend over time (monthly/yearly)
+ Country-wise revenue distribution
+ MOM % comparison between 2022 vs 2023 

2. Product Analysis 
+ Subcategory performance
+ Top 10 Profit Generating Product
+ Identification of low-margin products

3. Customer Analysis
+ Revenue by customer
+ Loyalty tier contribution
+ Customer segmentation insights

## 🔍 Key Business Insights
## 🌍 Revenue Distribution
+ Revenue is evenly distributed across countries (~11–13%)
+ Indicates low dependency on a single market

# 📦 Order Priority Insight
+ Low-priority orders contribute ~50% of total revenue
+ Suggests misalignment in priority classification or pricing strategy

## 🛍 Product Performance
+ Revenue is concentrated in a few subcategories (e.g., Utensils, Jackets)
+ Some categories (e.g., Audio) significantly underperform
+ Indicates portfolio imbalance

## 👥 Customer Insights
	• The majority of customers generate substantial revenue, indicating a strong high-value customer base

## 🎯 Loyalty Program Analysis
+ Bronze tier contributes ~60% of revenue
+ Indicates low customer progression to higher loyalty tiers	

## 💰 Profitability Analysis
+ Overall Gross Profit Margin ≈ 35.56%
+ Over 50% of products fall below average margin
+ Several products generate <10% margin
👉 Indicates margin leakage and pricing inefficiencies

## 📉 Seasonality Trend
+ Peak sales: December–January
+ Dip observed in February
+ Indicates seasonal demand pattern

## ⚙️ Channel & Priority Profitability
+ Profit margins are consistent across channels and order priorities
+ Suggests standardized pricing but missed optimization opportunities

## Product Efficiency Model (Key Highlight)
A custom Efficiency Score was developed:
Efficiency Score = Profit Contribution % / Cost Contribution %
## 📊 Product Classification:
+ ✅ Good Products: 84
+ ⚠️ Average Products: 51
+ ❌ Bad Products: 65
🧠 Insight:
+ Over 30% of products are inefficient
+ Many products consume resources without proportional returns

## 💡 Business Recommendations
## 🔴 Product Portfolio Optimization
+ Reduce or discontinue low-performing products. Focus on high-efficiency products

## 🟠 Pricing & Cost Optimization
+ Adjust pricing for low-margin products. Reduce cost through supplier optimization

## 🟡 Customer Strategy
+ Improve loyalty program to encourage tier upgrades. Focus on retaining high-value customers

## 🔵 Sales & Operations Optimization
+ Re-evaluate order priority strategy. Optimize channel-specific performance


## 📌 Conclusion
This project demonstrates how structured data analysis can:
+ Identify inefficiencies
+ Improve profitability
+ Support strategic decision-making
